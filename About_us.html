About Applied Communications

Established in 1956, Applied Communications Services, Inc. has offered its clients a wide variety of products, technologies and services for the communications industry. We are located in the New England area, but happily work with clients throughout the entire nation.

Since we’ve been in the industry for over 60 years, we have a strong reputation with the industry, our clients, and other companies we work alongside of providing precisely what our clients need.

We provide the clients we work with, the high quality services and products that they are in need of, regardless which wireless communications sectors those services and products come from, as well as offering some of the latest options in the communications industry, to ensure that your business is one that can run smoothly and effectively.

We provide turnkey performance, and have come a long way in recent years to ensure that we only work with the best in each industry sector of the wireless communication world. Since we only work with the best, we then only provide the best to you. This allows you to have dependable products and services that your customers can depend on.

Our over 200 combined years of industry experience allows us to provide each of our customers superior services, solutions, and full support. Our expertise is proven in both the technological and the communications sectors, ensuring every single one of our clients will get the specific services they need.

The services we provide include reliable RF solutions, efficient communication methods, and high quality products that cover both regional and national needs. This way you can track all of your company’s communication and business relationships.

We strive to provide only the highest and most efficient services within the industry. This allows each of our clients to feel confident with our services and products, because we fully understand how important it is that your company remain able to communicate with those inside your company, and the customers that make your company successful. We are always on top of industry standards, ensuring your satisfaction with the level of services and products we provide for you.
